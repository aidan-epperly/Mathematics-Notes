## Definition

Let $\mathcal{A}$ and $\mathcal{B}$ be [[von Neumann Algebra|von Neumann algebras]]. A map 
$$
\mathcal{E} : \mathcal{A} ^{\#} \to \mathcal{B} ^{\#}
$$
is *positive* if when $\rho \in \mathcal{A}^{\#}$ is positive then $\mathcal{E}(\rho)$ is positive as well.
