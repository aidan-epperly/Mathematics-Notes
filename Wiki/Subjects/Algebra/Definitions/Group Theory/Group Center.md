# Definition

Given a [[Group|group]] $G$, the *center* of $G$, denoted $Z(G)$, is the subgroup of elements $h \in G$ such that
$$
g h g^{-1}= h, \quad  \forall g\in G.
$$
Equivalently $Z(G)$ is the subgroup of all elements of $G$ that commute with $G$.
