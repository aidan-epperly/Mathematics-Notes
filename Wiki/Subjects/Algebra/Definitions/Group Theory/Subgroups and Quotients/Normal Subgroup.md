# Definition

Given a [[group]] $G$, a [[Subgroup|subgroup]] $H \subseteq G$ is called *normal* if the [[Group Coset|coset]] $gH$ is equal to the right [[Group Coset|coset]] $Hg^{-1}$ for all $g \in G$.
