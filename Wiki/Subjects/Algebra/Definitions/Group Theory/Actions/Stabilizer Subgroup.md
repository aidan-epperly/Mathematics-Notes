## Definition

Let $(G,S,\cdot)$ be a [[Group Action|group action]] and let $s \in S$. The set 
$$
G_{s} = \left\{ g \in  G \; \mid \; g\cdot s = s \right\},
$$
is called the *stabilizer subgroup* of $G$.
