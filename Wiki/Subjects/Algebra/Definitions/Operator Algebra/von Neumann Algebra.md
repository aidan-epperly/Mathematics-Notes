## Definition

A *von Neumann Algebra* $\mathcal{M}$ is a C\*-algebra equipped with a predual
$$
\mathcal{M}^{\#} \subseteq \mathcal{M}^{*}
$$ 
such that $\mathcal{M}^{\#*} = \mathcal{M}$.
