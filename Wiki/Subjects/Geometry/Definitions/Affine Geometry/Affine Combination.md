## Definition

Let $V$ be a vector space and let $x_{1},\dots,x_{n} \in V$. A linear combination 
$$
\gamma_{1} x_{1} + \dots + \gamma_{n} x_{n} \in  V
$$
is an *affine combination* if $\sum_{i} \gamma_{i} = 1$.
