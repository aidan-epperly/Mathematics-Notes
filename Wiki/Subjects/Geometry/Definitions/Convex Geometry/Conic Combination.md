## Definition

Let $V$ be a real [[Vector Space|vector space]] and $x_{1},\dots,x_{n} \in V$. A linear combination 
$$
\gamma_{1} x_{1} + \dots + \gamma_{n} x_{n} \in  V
$$
is called a *conic combination* if $\gamma_{i} \geq 0$.
